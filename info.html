UFAZ GPA CALCULATOR




The UFAZ GPA CALCULATOR was made by Ahmadov Kamal, Alasgarli Kamal, Gadirli Shams, Afandiyev Timur and supervisor Javid Khalilov as project for the L0 Study Project course. The purpose of the application is to provide UFAZ students with a quick and easy way to determine their GPAs before official results are released. To use the application enter your speciality, as well as the semester and get your average score!
